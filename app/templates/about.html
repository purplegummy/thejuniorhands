Our Commitment To Marginalized Groups

Unfortunately, many of the youth we serve belong to marginalized communities. These are communities that our society and governments have cast aside or deemed unworthy of attention. But it is in these very communities that the youth with the most potential reside. In order to provide more to those in need, we made the decision that all of our programs and opportunities be free of charge and open to all members of society, regardless of where they come from. Our organization is committed to providing an equal chance to all youth.

Vision Statement

The Junior Hands Association is a student-led organization that prides itself in fostering a better environment for education. We provide opportunities for high school students across Kentucky to connect with their communities and improve the lives of their fellow peers. Volunteers are encouraged to utilize their unique skill sets to better serve systematically disadvantaged communities. By emphasizing student-driven activism, we hope to improve the leadership, critical thinking, and creativity of our volunteers. With the goal of improving the situations that impair education, we hope to forge a more prepared generation of capable citizens.
